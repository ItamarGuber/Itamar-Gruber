Green Curry Paste

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp coriander seeds

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

  • Â½ tsp white pepper corns

  • 15 green Thai chilies

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 15 Thai basil leaves, julienned

  • 3 Tbsp lemongrass, thinly sliced, bottom part only

  • 1 Tbsp Galangal, finely chopped

  • 2 tsp kaffir lime zest

  • 2 tsp cilantro roots, finely chopped

  • 3 Tbsp shallots, finely chopepd

  • 2 Tbsp garlic, finely chopped

  • 1 tsp fermented shrimp paste

Tools

  • Mortar and pestle

Instructions

  1. Grind the dry spiced into a powder. Remove and set aside.

  2. Add chilies and salt to the mortar and pound until fine.

  3. Add the Thai basil leaves and pound until fine.

  4. If at any point the mixture feels too wet and slippery, add the dry spices to absorb excess moisture.

  5. Add lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime zest, and cilantro roots; pound until fine.

  6. Add shallots, garlic, and any remaining dry spices and pound into a fine paste.

  7. Add shrimp paste and pound to mix.
